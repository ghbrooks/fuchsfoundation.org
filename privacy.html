Privacy Policy

The Fuchs Foundation is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle any personal information we collect from you through our website.

We do not collect personal information about you unless you voluntarily provide it, for example by contacting us via email or through our contact page.

Any personal information you provide will only be used for the purpose for which it was given and will not be passed to third parties without your consent.

This website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, although this may affect the functionality of the site.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
