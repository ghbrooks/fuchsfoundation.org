Greenland 2016

The foundation launched its 5th expedition, the second to Greenland. Applications were invited from teachers in primary and secondary UK schools across all subjects.

The Fuchs Foundation aims to inspire teachers into developing both personally and professionally within their fields. Teachers must develop project work and shareable resources for local schools while giving regional talks to inspire students.

Fitness Requirements

Participants need adequate fitness for extended outdoor work. Fitness is assessed during training weekends with improvement guidance provided.

Application Process

Applicants complete forms and contact Bull Precision Expedition; suitable candidates receive training weekend reservations.

Training Weekend

Easter 2016 event in Peak District covering campcraft, equipment, fitness, fundraising, and project development - mandatory attendance required.
